Frequently Asked Questions

What can PDF Toolkit do?

PDF Toolkit lets you merge, split, compress, and convert PDFs directly in your browser. No file uploads to external servers — everything happens locally.

Are my files uploaded anywhere?

No. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Which browsers are supported?

PDF Toolkit works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers. Firefox support is planned.

The extension isn't working on a specific site. Why?

Some websites restrict browser extensions from running. Try using PDF Toolkit from its popup or options page instead. If the issue persists, let us know.

How do I merge multiple PDFs?

Click the PDF Toolkit icon in your browser toolbar, select "Merge," then drag and drop or select the files you want to combine. Reorder them if needed, then click "Merge."

Installation

  • Visit the Chrome Web Store and search for "PDF Toolkit by banshii"
  • Click "Add to Chrome"
  • The extension icon will appear in your toolbar
