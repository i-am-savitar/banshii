PDF Toolkit Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

banshii ("we", "our", "us") operates PDF Toolkit. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our browser extension.

Our Core Principle

Your files stay on your device. PDF Toolkit processes all files locally in your browser. We never upload, transmit, or store your PDF files on any server.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics: We collect anonymous data about which features are used (e.g., merge vs. split) to help prioritize development. This data contains no file content or personal information.
  • Error reports: If the extension encounters an error, an anonymous crash report may be sent to help us fix bugs.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your PDF files or their contents
  • Your browsing history
  • Personal identification information
  • File names or metadata

Permissions

PDF Toolkit requests only the browser permissions necessary to function. We do not access your browsing data or any information beyond what's required to process the PDFs you choose to open with the extension.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any user data to third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@banshii.com.
