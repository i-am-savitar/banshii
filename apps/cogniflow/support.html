CogniFlow Support

We're here to help you get the most out of CogniFlow.

Contact Us

If you're experiencing an issue or have a question, reach out to us at support@banshii.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

After installing CogniFlow, open the app and follow the onboarding walkthrough. It'll guide you through creating your first workspace and setting up your workflow.

Can I sync across devices?

Yes. CogniFlow syncs seamlessly across all your devices when you're signed in to the same account.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Data > Export. You can export your data in multiple formats including JSON and CSV.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at support@banshii.com with a description of the issue, the steps to reproduce it, and your device information. Screenshots are always helpful.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • Android 12 or later
  • An internet connection for sync features
