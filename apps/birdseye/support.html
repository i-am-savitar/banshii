Frequently Asked Questions

What is Birdseye?

Birdseye gives you a comprehensive overview of the information that matters most, presented in a clean, intuitive interface that updates in real time.

How do I customize my dashboard?

Tap the edit icon in the top right corner to enter customization mode. From there you can add, remove, and rearrange widgets to build your ideal overview.

Is my data stored locally or in the cloud?

Birdseye stores data locally on your device by default. Cloud sync is optional and can be enabled in Settings > Sync.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@banshii.com with details about the issue, steps to reproduce it, and your device info.

System Requirements
