Birdseye Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

banshii ("we", "our", "us") operates Birdseye. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address when you create an account (optional).
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Local data: Content you create within Birdseye is stored on your device by default.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To sync data across devices (when enabled)
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage analytics
  • To send important service updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous aggregate analytics may be used to improve our products.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. Local data remains on your device unless you opt in to cloud sync.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Contact privacy@banshii.com to exercise your rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@banshii.com.
