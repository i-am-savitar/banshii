High As A Clown Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

TL;DR — We don't collect your personal data. The app uses Game Center and AdMob, which are managed by Apple and Google respectively.

Data We Collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account or sign-up is required. Your gameplay stays on your device.

Game Center

The app uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple under their own Privacy Policy.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Reach out at j.cruiser@live.com.au and we'll get back to you.
